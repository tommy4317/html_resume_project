Twenty-five years ago, I once waited 3 days for an IT Tech to come fix the computer in my office. I couldn't get it to do anything, just a blank, black screen. When the Tech finally came to fulfill the work order, he listened patiently to my problem, then he reached over and pressed the big, silver, Dell button and it roared to life.
Now I can build websites using HTML, CSS, JS, and Java. Behind that I bring the years of management and organizational skills of a professional chef running some of the biggest kitchens across the country, and the open thinking, creative thought processes of an artist, musician, and published poet.
